The Solution

A New Foundation, Built on Community

Home Field Advantage Housing DAO is a decentralized, community-driven ecosystem. We leverage blockchain technology to fund, build, and govern affordable and sustainable housing communities based on Universal Design principles.

Affordable Housing Access

Find and secure affordable, high-quality housing options within our DAO-owned properties. Our model is designed to create permanently affordable homes, not speculative assets.

True Community Governance

As a DAO member, you have a real vote. Decide on new projects, land acquisitions, community rules, and how our treasury is spent using our fair quadratic voting models.

Integrated Digital Economy

Use our stablecoin (HFUSD) to pay rent, mortgages, or shop at local businesses within the community. Our economy is designed to be seamless, low-fee, and self-sustaining.

Universal & Smart Design

Our communities are built for everyone. With step-free entrances, smart home tech, and sensory-friendly layouts, we prioritize accessibility for all ages and abilities.

Sustainable & Green

We prioritize energy-efficient construction, renewable energy, green spaces, and local food systems. Live in a community that is as healthy for you as it is for the planet.

Fractional Asset Ownership

Through our asset token (HFHAT), members can own a fractional, liquid stake in the community's real estate and infrastructure. Build wealth as the community grows.

A Dual-Token Economy

Our ecosystem runs on two distinct tokens, creating a stable and balanced system for governance, investment, and daily transactions.

HFHAT (Home Field Advantage Token)

An asset-backed token representing fractional ownership in our real estate portfolio. Hold HFHAT to build wealth and participate in high-level governance.

HFUSD (Home Field USD)

A stablecoin used for all community transactions, such as paying rent, mortgage payments, and purchasing goods and services from local partners.

For Investors

A New Model for Investor Returns

Investing in the Home Field Advantage DAO is an opportunity to generate strong financial returns while creating measurable social impact. Our model offers multiple, transparent revenue streams.

Staking Rewards

Earn passive yield by staking your HFHAT tokens. The longer you commit to staking, the higher your Annual Percentage Yield (APY), rewarding long-term support for the ecosystem.

Rental Income

HFHAT token holders receive a proportional share of the net rental income generated from our entire portfolio of properties, distributed transparently via smart contract.

Ecosystem Fees

A small percentage of transaction fees from lease payments, mortgage processing, and commercial activity (using HFUSD) is collected and distributed back to HFHAT stakers.
